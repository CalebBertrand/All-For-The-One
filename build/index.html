Our mission is to love and lead people to a life-changing connection with Christ - and ALL for the ONE is about a specific aspect of how we’re going to keep doing that! I believe that if each of us would do our all for the one, we will see generations of people changed by the power and love of Jesus! So - I invite you to be a part of the ALL for the ONE initiative; let’s keep reaching our valley and beyond!
John Hansen, Lead Pastor
